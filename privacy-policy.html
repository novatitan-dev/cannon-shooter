PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

Cannon Shooter is designed to provide an engaging casual experience. We collect minimal information necessary to deliver and improve gameplay:

  • Local Data: Your custom username, game settings (such as music and volume), and high scores are saved locally on your device via storage.
  • Third-Party Analytics & Ads: We use services like Google AdMob to show advertising. These services may collect device identifiers and usage statistics to serve relevant ads.

2. How We Use Your Information

Collected information is used exclusively to:

  • Display and update your custom player name in the profile dashboard.
  • Synchronize and display your highest scores, game state, and statistics.
  • Deliver non-intrusive ad rewards, helping support the development of updates.

3. Data Security & Storage

We care about securing your choices. Because most details are kept locally on your device, clearing your app/browser cache will reset your offline progress and username. No sensitive personal data is uploaded to our servers.
